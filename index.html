A simple Bookmark
Manager

A clean and simple interface to organize your favourite
websites. Open a new tab and see your sites load
instantly. Try it for free.

Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your
favourite websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices
so you can acces them on the go

Bookmark in one click

Organize your bookmarks however you like. Our
simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete
control over how you manage your favourite sites.

Share your bookmarks

Easily share your bookmarks and collections with
other. Create a shareable link that you can send at
the click of a button.

Intelligent search

Our powerful search feature will help you find saved
sites in no time at all. No need to trawl through all
of your bookmarks.
